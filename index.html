About These Dashboards

These are fan-made, interactive music countdown dashboards created for exploration and enjoyment.

Each countdown lets you:

  • move through songs one at a time
  • listen to preview clips when available, or open the song in your music service
  • use the slider to see patterns across artists, years, albums, decades, and genres

How to use: drag the slider to move through the countdown, or tap a song in the “Last 10” list to jump ahead.

The dashboards work on desktop, tablet, and phone, with no login, tracking, or downloads required.

This is not an official WXPN project. It’s a community-minded, non-commercial effort shared in the spirit of music discovery. Song metadata and links are gathered from multiple sources, and not every song has a preview available.

Built as static, client-side dashboards · No tracking · No frameworks
